Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Race

World Tour cycling at its finest — through Geelong and along one of the world's most iconic coastlines.

calendar_month Late January / Early February location_on Geelong to Geelong (via Great Ocean Road) emoji_events UCI WorldTour
Official site → Find accommodation

info About the Race

Named in honour of Geelong's own Tour de France champion Cadel Evans, this UCI WorldTour race brings the world's elite cyclists to regional Victoria for a breathtaking one-day classic. Starting and finishing in Geelong, the route winds through the Surf Coast, climbs Challambra, and follows the spectacular Great Ocean Road — making it one of the most scenic and challenging races on the global calendar.
