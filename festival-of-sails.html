info About the Festival

The Festival of Sails is one of the Southern Hemisphere's premier sailing events, drawing competitors and spectators from across Australia and beyond each Australia Day long weekend. Held from the Royal Geelong Yacht Club on the shores of Corio Bay, the festival combines world-class racing with a massive free public program — live music, food stalls, kids activities, and a spectacular waterfront atmosphere.
